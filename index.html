Tree Planting

The Nature Team of University of Moratuwa conducts various activities with the student participation, increase their knowledge to make more environmental concerned scholars in future with the vision of “Sustainable development through exploring & understanding nature”. As a part of this attempt, we commemorated the National Tree planting event by initiating Endemic and indigenous plant species for University orchard, proposed under the master proposal of University landscape development. The event was held at open air theater (Lagaan) premises with participation of honorable guests including Deputy Vice chancellor, academic and non-academic staff and the members of Nature team as well as students of university, representing all faculties.We planted Mangifera zeylanica (Etamba) and Mimusops elengi, (Muna Mal) plants to symbolize this event. This event supported to generate environmental awareness of university students and also to improve the landscape quality of university premises.

Beach cleaning campaign - Mount Lavinia

With the intention of making the environment better, Synopsys Sri Lanka family came together with the Nature Team of University of Moratuwa to host a cleaning campaign on the Mount Lavinia beach. The event was successfully completed with everyone working hard together to clean up the beach. The initiative was well appreciated by the resident of the area and the Municipal Council. The team also distributed garbage bins to help the community keep their environment clean.
